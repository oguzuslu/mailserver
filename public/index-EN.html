About Spinigo

Why should I use Spinigo?

It enables businesses of all sizes to promote and reach the right customer base thanks to artificial intelligence support.

On the user's side, thanks to the artificial intelligence support in the application, it provides a social platform experience that offers business suggestions according to their preferences and enables them to benefit from the campaigns, while being fun thanks to the game module inside, on the other hand, it increases socialization.

App Features

Our features that bring businesses and users together

USER

Free Download

You can download the app for free from the App Store and Google PLAY. You can benefit from the campaigns around you as you wish, without paying any fee.

Spin the wheel

Use the spin rights defined in your daily account. Discover new places and benefit from special gifts with artificial intelligence.

Generate QR Code

When you spin the wheel, go to the specified business and have the QR code of the gift special for you read.

Get Your Gift

You can get the gift by going to the business and scanning the QR code. You can also send your gift to a friend or donate it to a foundation.

SHOP

Free Download

You can download the mobile application for free from App Store or Google Play. You can access your data and manage your gifts and discounts from the Web-based panel specially prepared for you.

Scan QR Code

With the QR code reader in the mobile application, you can read the campaign QR code your customer has won in a few seconds and complete your transaction.

Manage Campaigns and Products

You can easily examine your customer structure from within the application graphically, as well as manage your gifts and discounts.

Reach Audiences

Deliver the campaigns you organize to the right audiences with the help of artificial intelligence in the application.
